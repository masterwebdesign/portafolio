Who I Am

I'm a passionate, purpose-driven, UX/UI Developer and Designer that is happily married to the most wonderful woman in the world. Shameless plug, she's an outstanding photographer and you can view her work here. I'm also a coffee enthusiast that loves to solve problems and find solutions. Lastly, but most importantly, I serve a gracious, forgiving, and loving Jesus.

I'd like to break down "purpose-driven". I’m passionate about developing an online presence for individuals and companies to give them exactly what they need in the crazy digital world we live in. For me, it's far more so than just building a website, web app, or developing a feature. To simply be able to serve someone and give them an opportunity to live a better and more efficient life from what I’ve developed...that is the entire reason why I started creating and coding in the first place.

Outside of any freelance work, I thoroughly enjoy contributing to open source and working on personal projects. I also work full-time at a medical supply company as a Frontend Developer where I get to work on a really diverse software focused on modern native javascript, vue.js, php, laravel, and twig.

Take a look around and feel free to contact me with any requests or questions!
